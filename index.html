LiquidNote

A minimal glass note for macOS.

What it does

Always ready

Opens instantly to a clean, distraction-free surface.

Auto-save

Your notes save as you type and as you switch between them.

Make it yours

Glass appearance, colors, gradient borders, glow, fonts.

Multi-window

Open several notes side by side with ⇧⌘N.

Search

Find anything across every note with ⌘F.

Private by design

Notes never leave your Mac. No account, no network.
