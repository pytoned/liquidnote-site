Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

LiquidNote is built to respect your privacy completely. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Data we collect

None. LiquidNote does not collect, store on our servers, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Your notes

Every note you create is stored locally on your Mac and never leaves your device. We have no access to your notes and no ability to read them.

Network access

LiquidNote does not connect to the internet. It requires no account, no sign-in, and no network connection to function.

Third parties

LiquidNote includes no third-party SDKs, analytics, or services.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact hi@and.rs.
